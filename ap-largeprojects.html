what we do

Asian Paints Project Sales is a growing division of Asian Paints, which caters to the needs of large users such as co-operative housing societies, new buildings, institutes, government organization/PSUs etc., with presence in 63 locations including all metros in the country. With the help of our strong dealer network, Asian Paints Project Sales has the ability to service painting requirements across all cities in India and has a range of services to offer including technical assistance, sampling, foresite and on-site visit.

services

sampling

Our expert painters will visit your site and do a sample of the shades you have selected. This will not only help you get a better idea of how your chosen shades actually look but will also allow you to get a feel of the product you have selected for your site.

technologist

A perfectly painted surface is dependent on many factors paint being one of them. Our on-field technologists will help you get the optimum results by conducting regular inspections, checking the substrate conditions and providing an estimate of paint consumption.

foresite

The foresite service can enable you to arrive at the most appropriate and pleasing colours for your building. Our personnel will click an image of your building and will give you 4 sets of colour combinations which will make the colour selection process a lot simpler for you.

onsite

Our on-site services include the introduction of premium contractors for application, coordination vis--vis all painting needs and regular site visits by our personnel.
